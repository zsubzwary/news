### Economy
- The federal government has unveiled a Rs18.8 trillion budget for FY2026-27, targeting a 4% GDP growth and projecting average inflation at 8.2%. *[Dawn](https://www.dawn.com/news/2007283/govt-unveils-rs188tr-budget-for-fy2026-27-gdp-growth-targeted-at-4pc)*
- A defence budget of approximately Rs3 trillion has been proposed, a 17.6% increase to address growing security concerns. *[Dawn](https://www.dawn.com/news/2007456/budget-2026-27-defence-gets-rs3tr-amid-security-concerns)*
- Tax relief measures have been introduced for the salaried class, businesses, and property buyers/sellers. *[Dawn](https://www.dawn.com/news/2007489/budget-offers-tax-break-to-salaried-class-businesses)* *[ARY](https://arynews.tv/filers-incentives-in-budget-2026-27)*

- The government set a privatization target of Rs160.5 billion for the upcoming fiscal year. *[Business Recorder](https://www.brecorder.com/news/40425327/rs1605bn-sell-off-target-set)*
- New tax provisions include a proposed withholding tax on income derived from social media platforms. *[Business Recorder](https://www.brecorder.com/news/40425326/tax-proposed-on-income-from-social-media-platforms)*
- Customs duties have been cut on 7,500 industrial raw materials and machinery items to support industry. *[ARY](https://arynews.tv/pakistan-government-cuts-duties-on-raw-materials)*
- The development budget includes Rs3.675 trillion, with CPEC 2.0 identified as the primary new project. *[Dawn](https://www.dawn.com/news/2007461/budget-2026-27-cpec-20-only-new-project-in-rs36tr-development-kitty)*
- The FPCCI criticized the budget for lacking sufficient measures to reduce industrial production costs. *[Business Recorder](https://www.brecorder.com/news/40425328/budget-lacks-measures-to-boost-production-fpcci)*
